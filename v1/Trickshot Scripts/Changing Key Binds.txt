Wanna set a script's INTIAL KEY to another?

*FIND THE DESIRED SCRIPT*

Right Click on script and find Edit Script.
Look at the very top for something [EX]: - 3:: OR ~*Z::
Replace existing key (3::) with (?::) desired key.
SAVE.
Double click script again (to refresh it)
